More about the cultivation process

Sour cherries come from our orchards spread over 150 hectares across the region of Vojvodina, ensuring uniform quality and structure of the final product. We also receive part of the fruit from our subcontractors, with whom we maintain excellent cooperation in cultivation and protection processes, ensuring only the highest quality raw materials for our processing unit.

IQF sour cherries are produced from healthy, whole, and fresh fruits of uniform quality, without impurities. The cherries undergo processes such as washing, sorting, calibrating, and pitting.
Sour cherries can be used in various ways. Eat them fresh from the freezer, or use them to create a juicy pie, a variety of desserts, cakes, juices, or jams.
